RULES

In 3D Tic-Tac-Toe, players take turns placing their marks (X or O) on a 3x3x3 grid. The goal is to get three of your marks in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally across any of the three layers, or diagonally through the layers. The game continues until one player achieves this goal or the grid is full, resulting in a draw.
